About Me

Similar to every Indian kid, I spent most of my childhood playing Cricket (read gully-cricket). Computers had my interest for a long time, and technology has always fascinated me. So, a kid who was always told to be a doctor gradually became interested in becoming an engineer.

I love doing competitive programming, and nothing makes me happier than seeing 'All Correct' on a submission. I'm currently an 'expert' on Codeforces and have a 4 star rating on Codechef.

My specialties include problem-solving, a firm grasp of data structures and algorithms, and an everlasting eagerness to learn something new. I have a strong grip on Python and Java, and working knowledge of C++, PL/SQL, HTML and CSS. I'm currently learning Node.js, Express.js and MongoDB, and yearning to try my hand at more programming languages and frameworks.

Listening to true-crime podcasts and reading sci-fi novels have been my favourite time-killers in this seemingly never-ending pandemic. Hit me up to discuss why test match is the greatest format of Cricket.
